Who am I?

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of Ottawa, specializing in Data Science and minoring in Life Science. My background includes web development, data analysis, and full-stack projects that combine clean design with functional code. I've built finance tools, face recognition systems, and even real-time chat servers all with a strong focus on user experience and practical outcomes.

When I’m not writing code or debugging something late at night, I love spending time outdoors hiking new trails and capturing nature through my camera. I’m also an avid reader, especially of historical fiction. Whether it’s exploring a new landscape or a new idea, I’m always curious and driven by a love for learning and creativity.
